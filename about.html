"If what we use every day is made with art, and not thrown together by chance or caprice, then we shall have nothing to hide"
- Bruno Munari

Bruno Munari was a Itailan designer, artist, inventor and general badass. His 1966 book Design as Art is to me as relevent to today as it was the the day it was published, and a must read for designs folks. Munari talks of design as something that must be beautiful, functional and accessible something we strive to accomplish. This book almost reads as a blog, with ideas spanning aross various subjects and topics, musings on the design world. Orignally pulished as newpaper articles Design as Art gives insight to Munaris thoughts on the design world and how we can all achive it.
